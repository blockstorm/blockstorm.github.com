Donate to Blockstorm

Below you can donate to the server. Currently we are only accepting donations through paypal but you don't need a paypal account if you have a credit or bank card. If you are unable to pay using one of the methods paypal provides please contact admin[at]blockstorm[dot]com.
After donating 5€ (about 6.50$ or 4£) your rank on the server will be changed to Donor. Because this doesn't happen automatically it might take a while until an admin promotes you. If you aren't promoted within 24 hours just contact admin[at]blockstorm[dot]com.
See the ranks page for the permissions Donors have.

All donations are solely used to pay for the hosting. Thank you for supporting the server.
